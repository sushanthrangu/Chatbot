HOW TO PLAY

1

The game is played on a 4×4×4 grid (3D space)

2

Players alternate placing X and O in empty cells

3

First to get 4 in a row wins (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)

4

Lines can go across layers (3D diagonals count!)

5

Full board with no winner = Tie game
