Refund Policy — EventHub (v1.0, effective Jan 1, 2025)

Summary
- 100% refund up to 14 days before the event
- 50% refund from 13 to 7 days before the event
- No refunds within 6 days of the event
- Name transfers allowed until 24 hours before the event
- Non-refundable items: processing fees, add-on workshops once attended

Details
1) Eligibility
   - Refunds apply only to tickets purchased through EventHub or official partners.
   - Group bookings are treated as a single order unless split at purchase time.

2) Deadlines
   - T−14d to event start: 100% refund (excludes processing fees).
   - T−13d to T−7d: 50% refund.
   - T−6d to event start: no refund.

3) Exceptions
   - Event cancellation or date change > 30 days: 100% refund including fees.
   - Verified medical emergency: case-by-case review (documentation required within 7 days).
   - Severe weather forcing venue closure: full refund or credit.

4) Fees & Credits
   - Processing fees are non-refundable except if EventHub cancels.
   - Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.
   - Store credit valid 12 months, single-use, non-transferable.

5) Transfers & Resale
   - Name transfer permitted until 24h before event at no cost via My Tickets → Transfer.
   - Resale must use official resale link to preserve ticket validity.

6) How to Request
   - Go to My Tickets → Select Order → Request Refund.
   - Or email support@eventhub.example with Order ID, attendee name, and reason.

7) Contact
   - Support hours: Mon–Fri 9am–6pm ET, Sat 10am–2pm ET
