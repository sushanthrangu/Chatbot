## Entry & Security
- Valid ticket and government-issued photo ID required.
- All guests subject to security screening (bag check/metal detection).
- Re-entry may be restricted; check event page for details.

## Prohibited Items
- Weapons, illegal substances, fireworks, outside alcohol.
- Professional cameras, drones, or recording gear without media pass.
- Large bags > 12" x 6" x 12"; clear bags preferred.

## Accessibility (ADA)
- Wheelchair-accessible entrances, seating, and restrooms available.
- Service animals permitted (no pets). Contact support for accommodations 72h in advance.

## Food & Beverage
- Outside food/beverage generally not allowed (medical exceptions with proof).
- Alcohol service ends 30 minutes before event end; 21+ ID required.

## Safety & Conduct
- Follow staff instructions at all times. Harassment or unsafe behavior leads to removal without refund.
- Smoking/vaping only in designated outdoor areas.

## Parking & Transit
- On-site parking is limited; consider rideshare or public transit.
- Arrive 30 minutes early for security and seating.

## Lost & Found
- Report items at Guest Services. Items kept 14 days then donated/disposed.
