Event Handbook — Organizer & Attendee Guide (v1.0)

1) Registration & Tickets
- Sales channels: EventHub web, mobile app, official partner embeds.
- Ticket types: General Admission, VIP, Student, Group (min 4), Early-bird.
- Limits: 6 tickets per customer unless whitelisted by organizer.
- Anti-fraud: device and payment risk checks; suspicious orders placed on manual review.

2) RSVP & Check-in
- RSVP holds do not guarantee entry without completed payment.
- Check-in methods: QR scan, lookup by email/last name, manual override with ID.
- Offline mode: Check-in app caches attendee list; sync when online returns.

3) Payments & Invoicing
- Supported: cards, wallets, UPI (IN), bank transfer (EU), pay-on-site (limited).
- Invoices auto-sent; re-download from My Tickets.
- Refunds follow Refund Policy; partial refunds allowed until T−6d.

4) Cancellations & Changes
- Postpone: notify all attendees via email/push; ticket remains valid.
- Cancel: full refund including fees unless caused by third-party venue closure.
- Lineup changes allowed; disclose prominently on event page.

5) Code of Conduct
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination.
- Security may remove attendees breaching conduct; no refunds for removals.

6) Communications
- Pre-event: schedule reminders at T−7d, T−48h, T−4h.
- On-site: use push/onsite signage for last-minute changes.
- Post-event: send thank-you + feedback survey within 48h.

7) Data & Privacy
- PII stored encrypted; access restricted to authorized staff.
- Export attendee data via Organizer Dashboard (CSV).

8) Support
- Hours: Mon–Fri 9am–6pm ET; on-call on event days.
- Escalations: safety/security issues prioritized over general inquiries.
